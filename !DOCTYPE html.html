About Me

I am a senior researcher with a Ph.D. in biotechnology and over 9 years of experience in culturing, biochemical, and genomic characterization of diverse microorganisms, especially bacteria. My expertise lies in molecular biology techniques, sequencing, and bioinformatics.

Research

My research focuses on microbial community structure and interactions in agricultural soil, utilizing bioinformatics tools and advanced molecular techniques. I have significant experience in NGS data analysis and CRISPR/Cas9 gene editing pipelines.
